What is included in each cleaning type

Every visit covers standard wiping, dusting, vacuuming and mopping. The level of detail changes based on the type of clean you choose.

First-time and deep cleans
  • Detailed dusting of high and low surfaces in every room.
  • Thorough wipe of kitchen counters, backsplash and exterior of appliances.
  • Scrub of sinks, tubs, showers, toilets and bathroom counters.
  • Wipe of baseboards, door frames and light switch plates in main areas.
  • Make beds, straighten rooms and tidy visible surfaces.
Standard maintenance cleans
  • General dusting of furniture, décor and easily reached surfaces.
  • Wipe of kitchen counters, stovetop and exterior of appliances.
  • Clean bathroom sinks, mirrors, toilets and tub or shower touchups.
  • Vacuum and mop of main living areas, bedrooms and hallways.
  • Empty small trash cans and reset spaces so the home feels fresh.
Move-in and move-out cleans
  • Top to bottom dusting of empty rooms, shelves and ledges.
  • Wipe inside cabinets and drawers and clean closet shelves.
  • Thorough cleaning of kitchen counters, sinks and exterior of appliances.
  • Extra attention to bathrooms so they are move-in ready.
  • Baseboards, doors and high-touch surfaces throughout the home.
